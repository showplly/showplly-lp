// The starting question

Why do we trust Amazon?

Because Amazon stands between
buyers and sellers.
Without the intermediary — trust disappears.

Every major e-commerce platform operates on the same fundamental model: the platform stands in the middle. Buyers trust the intermediary. Sellers depend on the intermediary. The platform extracts 15–30% for this service — forever.

What if trust could travel with the content itself?
No marketplace intermediary. Just verified identity, embedded in the content — before it is posted. The content becomes self-verifiable. Trust travels with the content, not through a central platform.

This is the question Showplly was built to answer. And the reason a patented identity layer now exists.

// Core concept

Content with Identity.

Today's content is fingerprint-less. A product photo on Instagram carries no proof of origin, no proof of authenticity, no proof that the person posting it has the right to sell it.

Today — fingerprint-less content
A product photo is just a photo.
It has pixels. It has likes. It has reach. But it has no verifiable connection to the physical product it represents. Anyone can copy it, repost it, sell counterfeits through it. The platform is the only thing standing between the image and fraud — and platforms fail at this daily.
Trust lives in the platform, not in the content.
Showplly — identity-bearing content
A product photo that proves itself.
Showplly embeds a verified identity layer directly into the content — before it is posted. The content carries two proofs: that this represents a real, authentic product, and that the person posting it has the commercial right to sell it. These proofs travel with the content everywhere it goes.
Trust lives in the content itself.

// The core thesis

Not a Verification Layer.

Many technologies can confirm whether content or a product is authentic. That confirmation, by itself, changes nothing. Showplly is built around what happens immediately after — and that is the part nobody else does.

Action is the missing layer.
Verification is not the goal.
Verification is an input for Action.

Depending on the verification result, commerce can proceed, be restricted, or be stopped — automatically, before any transaction occurs. Only authorized actions are allowed to continue.

✅
Proceed
Identity and authorization confirmed. The transaction continues to Checkout without interruption.
⚠️
Restrict
Partial verification. The transaction may proceed with limitations — e.g. region, quantity, or additional confirmation required.
⛔
Stop
Verification fails. The transaction is halted before Checkout. No sale occurs. The event is still recorded in the Audit layer.
// The full flow

From post to profit.
End to end.

Eight events. One continuous flow. No platform intermediary. This is what Showplly's PoC will demonstrate — end to end, in real time.

1
🏭
Content Creation
A maker's product is photographed and processed into clean, professional content — ready for identity embedding.
Inputs: Product makers · Content creation tools
2
🔐
Identity Layer
Core patent
The patented identity layer embeds two verifiable proofs into the content: product authenticity (this content represents a genuine product) and commercial authorization (this seller has the right to sell). This happens before posting. The content becomes self-verifying.
Technology: Patent pending · PCT filed — details under NDA only
3
📱
SNS Post
The seller — any individual who has received verified Identity Content — posts to Instagram, X, or TikTok. No store setup. No platform registration. No barrier. The act of posting is the act of selling.
Channels: Instagram · X/Twitter · TikTok
4
✅
Verification
Core patent
When a buyer accesses the content, verification executes automatically. The content proves itself — both product authenticity and seller authorization — in seconds, without any marketplace as intermediary.
Output: VERIFIED ✓ — Authentic · Authorized
5
⚡
Action
Verification itself is not the goal. Verification becomes an input for action. Depending on the result, commerce can proceed, be restricted, or be stopped. Only authorized actions are allowed to continue — automatically, before any transaction occurs.
Outcomes: Proceed · Restrict · Stop
6
🛒
Transaction
Only when Action allows it does the buyer proceed to Shopify Checkout. No redirect to a marketplace. No third-party platform interception. The content was the storefront. Shopify processes the transaction as the trusted payment infrastructure.
Infrastructure: Shopify Checkout · Shopify Payments
7
💰
Distribution
Revenue is automatically distributed to the maker and the seller according to pre-set ratios — with no platform extracting 30%. Distribution is automatic, transparent, and immediate. Both parties know exactly what they receive and why.
Infrastructure: Stripe Connect · Automated distribution
8
📋
Audit
Every event in the transaction — content creation, identity embedding, SNS post, verification, the Action decision, transaction, and distribution — is timestamped and recorded in an immutable audit log. The entire commerce event, including why an Action decision was made, lives in the record.
Infrastructure: Audit log · Full traceability
