Pre-Financial Identity & Authenticity Layer

Identity before tokenization, finance, and autonomous execution.

Showplly is not a marketplace and not a payment system. It is a pre-financial identity infrastructure for physical and digital assets — a shared layer that establishes what an asset is, that it is authentic, and how its provenance and ownership can be verified, before any financialization or automation occurs.

The Three-Layer Stack

1. AI Agents / Web3 Applications (Execution Layer)
  • Autonomous agents, smart contracts, Web3 applications
  • Require trusted identity, provenance, and ownership signals
  • Can only execute safely when “what is real” is cryptographically anchored
2. Showplly Identity Layer (Pre-Financial Core)
  • Product Identity Anchor (unique, persistent asset identity)
  • Pre-Financial Identity Anchor (before custody, settlement, or token issuance)
  • Native Provenance & Ownership Graph (verifiable lifecycle history)
  • PCT international patent filed for core identity and provenance structures
3. Physical Goods Layer (Reality Layer)
  • Manufacturers, logistics, distribution, customs, compliance
  • Real-world goods, digital twins, and cross-border supply chains
  • Where fraud, mis-declaration, and authenticity disputes originate

Positioning

Showplly occupies the missing middle layer: not financial infrastructure, and not application logic, but identity and authenticity as social infrastructure.

This layer is vendor-neutral and chain-agnostic. Enterprise integrations (e.g., Oracle, supply chain systems, customs, RWA platforms) are implementation examples, not the philosophical center.
