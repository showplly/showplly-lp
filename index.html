Today's EC
works against you.

01

// How it works

Content becomes
the verified store.

Showplly embeds an identity and verification layer directly into content — before it is posted. The content itself becomes the proof.

1
🏭
Product Creation
A maker's product is photographed and processed through a content creation partner. Professional, clean, ready for identity embedding.
Inputs
Product Makers Content Creation Tools
2
🔐
Identity Layer
The patented identity layer embeds product authenticity and commercial authorization into the content. This happens before posting. The content becomes self-verifying.
Technology
Patent Pending PCT Filed Oracle ADB
3
📱
SNS Post
The seller posts Identity Content to Instagram, X, or TikTok. No store setup. No registration. No barrier. Just post.
Channels
Instagram X / Twitter TikTok
4
✅
Verification
When a buyer accesses the content, verification executes automatically. The content proves itself — both product authenticity and seller authorization — in seconds, without any marketplace in between.
Output
VERIFIED ✓ Authentic Authorized
5
⚡
Action
Verification is not the goal — it's the input. Depending on the result, commerce can proceed, be restricted, or be stopped automatically. Only authorized actions are allowed to continue.
Outcomes
Proceed Restrict Stop
6
🛒
Checkout → Earn
When Action allows, the buyer proceeds to Shopify Checkout. Purchase completes. Profit distributes automatically to maker and seller. Every event is audit-logged.
Infrastructure
Shopify Stripe Connect Audit
